Play for the Planet

We provide safe, earth-friendly playthings for kids and families as part of a solution for a more sustainable future. Nurturing young minds and bright futures through joyful products made free of harm to children and the planet.

Hello!

With a name like Green Toys, you better believe we’re passionate about the earth and its children. Social and environmental responsibility drive our passion for unquestionably safe and playful products for kids and families.

We started small, garage-small, in 2007, with what seemed like a little idea and a whole lot of passion. We developed a process for turning milk jugs into 100% BPA-free recycled plastic, and then on to recyclable kid goods. Today, we lead the earth-friendly toy industry, continuously experimenting, innovating and perfecting our technique for the best products possible.

Thank you for supporting Green Toys and a playful planet.

Our Community

We’re only as good as the company we keep, so we’ve partnered with organizations that help us take what we believe in at Green Toys one-step further.

Healthy Child, Healthy World

Healthy Child Healthy World is a California non-profit organization whose programs demand corporate accountability, engage communities for action, support safer chemicals and products, and influence regulatory reform.

Center for Childhood Creativity

The Center for Childhood Creativity, a program of the Bay Area Discovery Museum, pioneers research, thought-leadership, and training programs that inspire innovation, imagination, and creative thinking in a child’s early years.

Sustainable Surf

Sustainable Surf is dedicated to protecting the ocean playground and engaging communities to solve the most pressing environmental issues facing our oceans.

NatureBridge

NatureBridge fosters environmental literacy to sustain our planet, providing hands-on environmental science programs for children and teens in the magnificence of nature’s classroom. Students are immersed in the wonder and science of our national parks in Yosemite, Golden Gate, Olympic, Santa Monica Mountains, Channel Islands, and Prince William Forest.
